"Alzheimer's disease is named after Dr. Alois Alzheimer, a German psychiatrist and pathologist who first described the disease in 1906 after observing changes in the brain tissue of a patient who died with unusual mental illness. While Dr. Alzheimer made the initial discovery and description, numerous researchers and developers have since contributed to understanding and developing treatments for Alzheimer's disease".
