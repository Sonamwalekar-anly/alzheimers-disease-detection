Key Features of Healthy Brain Function

Memory

Normal age-related changes only

Cognitive Testing

Scores within normal range for age

Daily Function

Fully independent in all activities

Risk Level

Annual conversion to MCI: 1-2%

Recommended Prevention Strategies

  • Physical Exercise: 150+ minutes of aerobic activity weekly
  • Brain Nutrition: Mediterranean or MIND diet rich in antioxidants
  • Cognitive Stimulation: Regular mental challenges and learning
  • Social Engagement: Meaningful social connections
  • Quality Sleep: 7-8 hours nightly with good sleep hygiene
  • Stress Management: Mindfulness and relaxation practices
  • Vascular Health: Control blood pressure, cholesterol, and blood sugar
